Who We Are PawStay ("we", "us", "our") is a pet sitting marketplace platform that connects pet owners with trusted pet sitters across Canada. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website pawstay.ca and any related services. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@pawstay.ca. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information you provide directly Account information: First name, last name, email address, phone number, password Sitter profile: City, neighborhood, years of experience, services offered, rates, biography, profile photo Booking information: Check-in/check-out dates, pet type, messages between pet owners and sitters Reviews: Star ratings, written reviews, photos of pets you upload 2.2 Information collected automatically Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform Device information: Browser type, operating system, IP address Cookies: Session cookies for authentication; preference cookies for currency (CAD/USD) and language (EN/FR) 2.3 Payment information We do NOT store credit card numbers or banking details. All payments are processed securely by PayPal and Stripe. We only receive transaction confirmation (success/failure) and the amount paid. 3. How We Use Your Information We use your personal information to: Provide our services: Match pet owners with sitters, process bookings and payments Send notifications: Booking confirmations, new reservation requests, review alerts via email Improve the platform: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience Ensure safety: Verify sitter profiles, detect and prevent fraud Communicate: Send important updates about your account or our services Legal compliance: Meet obligations under Canadian law (PIPEDA) We do NOT sell your personal information. We do NOT use your data for third-party advertising. 4.
